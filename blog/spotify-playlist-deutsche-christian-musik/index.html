Spotify Playlist: Deutsche Christian Musik

March 13, 2020 - 1 min read

In the winter and spring of 2016, I was privileged enough to join the Abilene Christian University Honor's College to Study Abroad in Leipzig, Germany. While there, I was able to travel and experience a myriad of locales, from Bach's Thomaskirche and Athens, Greece, to even a trip to London, Scotland, and Ireland during holiday.

While visiting a contemporary church with friends, I was able to talk to the lead worship singer, who spoke extremely fluent English. He told me that he what had helped him pick up a new language was taking vocabulary from English-sung Contemporary Christian Music and listening to the songs on repeat. In order to reverse-engineer (to much less success, I must admit) this, I've curated the below playlist. Enjoy.
